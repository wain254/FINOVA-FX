About this tool: PulseBot is a third-party interface built with the Deriv API. Until you click Connect Deriv Account, everything runs on simulated data so you can try the builder safely. Once connected, prices are streamed live from Deriv and trades placed here use real contract execution on the Deriv account you authorize (demo or real, depending on which account you select) — they carry the same financial risk as trading directly on Deriv, including the possibility of losing your full stake. Money-management blocks (Martingale, Stop Loss, Take Profit) are convenience logic run locally in your browser; they do not eliminate risk. This app is not operated by Deriv and is not financial advice.
